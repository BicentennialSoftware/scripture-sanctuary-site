Privacy Policy for Scripture Sanctuary

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Scripture Sanctuary respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Scripture Sanctuary does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information or personally identifiable data from users.

The app does not require account creation and does not gather information such as name, email address, phone number, location data, contacts, photos, payment information, usage analytics, or advertising identifiers.

Local Device Data

Scripture Sanctuary may save certain app preferences and reading-related information locally on your device only in order to support app functionality, including bookmarks, reading progress, and app settings or preferences.

This information stays on your device and is not sent to the developer.

Third-Party Services

Scripture Sanctuary does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services to collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Scripture Sanctuary does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any users.

Data Sharing

Because Scripture Sanctuary does not collect personal data, no personal data is sold, shared, rented, or disclosed to third parties.

Security

Since Scripture Sanctuary does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no user personal information stored on external servers by the developer.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by posting the revised version with a new effective date.
